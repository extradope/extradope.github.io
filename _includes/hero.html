Extra Dope

The sounds of the boom bap.
Fat laces on the kicks.
Scruffed up linoleum.
The screech of permanent markers
and vapors from aerosol paint.
Crates of records,
stacks of cassette tapes.

This was that culture.

They refer to it the Golden Age,
but for me,
it was more than just a time and place.
It passed through my veins,
it provided an escape.

These collections of recordings
dive into how my life was affected,
inspired,
and ultimately transformed
by hip hop culture.

These are my tapes...

This is my story.
